Consulting

Michael Schmuker

I advise organizations and teams on high-impact challenges in data science and AI, combining deep technical expertise with senior science management experience.

Typical engagements include technical reviews, strategy and readiness work, evaluation and benchmarking, and advisory support for data governance and responsible AI.

I bring hands-on experience in securing academic funding (ca. 3M€) and in reviewing competitive proposals—insight that helps teams design evaluation-ready projects.

I work with research-driven organizations, R&D teams, and public-sector initiatives. If you’d like to explore a collaboration, feel free to get in touch.
